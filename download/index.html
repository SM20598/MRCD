Alley Loop (A II)

Alley Loop extends the trajectory of Alley Fast and is the only bi-directional sequence of MRCD. Here, the robot start in the main campus area before driving up the same steep path as in Alley Fast. At the very North of the campus, the robot turns and follows the same path as in Alley Loop. Afterwards, the robot enters a particularly narrow pathway, where it is forced to take a handful of sharp turns to reach the campus center. Here, the robot turns and follows the same trajectory as before but in opposite direction, until it reaches its starting location. Alley loop features similar characteristics to Alley Fast but offers further challenges in serpentine locomotion, further height variations, and narrow environments in addition to the open environments.

Town Clockwise (T I)

Town Clockwise captures many challenging areas of the campus environment. The sequence starts in the main campus area before driving up the same path as in Alley Fast and Alley Loop, but takes a right-turn after. After driving on cobblestone surface, the robot varying environment characteristics, such as long narrow paths between tall buildings, uneven terrain, strong height differences, common obstacles (bikes, cars, trees), bright sunlight, and wide open and closed spaces.
